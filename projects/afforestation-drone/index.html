Autonomous Afforestation Drone with Robotic Manipulator

A hexacopter-mounted robotic arm for autonomous soil analysis and precision seed sowing, featuring a novel suspension system for soil interaction stability. Patent No. 549953.

System Design

  • Designed and fabricated a lightweight robotic arm in Fusion 360 for soil analysis and seed sowing.
  • Integrated the arm with a hexacopter using ArduPilot for autonomous flight and target-site positioning.
  • Developed a novel suspension system to maintain stability during soil interaction.
  • Built a controlled testbed to validate sowing accuracy under variable terrain and moisture conditions.
